Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how BeapShare ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our image sharing service.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email address, authentication data)
  • Images you upload and their metadata
  • Usage data (access times, pages viewed, and the routes by which you access our service)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To store and serve your uploaded images
  • To communicate with you about service updates
  • To prevent abuse and ensure security
  • To improve our service based on usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using Firebase services. Your images are stored in Firebase Storage, and user data is stored in Firebase Authentication and Firestore. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • When you explicitly share images using our sharing features
  • With service providers who assist in operating our service
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Discord community.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
